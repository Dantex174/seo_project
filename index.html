What is RTP and why is it important?

RTP is an important factor to consider when choosing which casino games to play, as it gives an indication of the potential payout that players can expect. However, it is important to note that RTP is calculated over a long period of time and does not guarantee any particular outcome for individual sessions of play.

RTP stands for Return to Player. It is a term used to describe the percentage of money wagered on a casino game that is expected to be returned to the player over time.

For example, if a casino game has an RTP of 95%, it means that over the long term, for every $100 wagered on the game, the player can expect to receive $95 in winnings, with the remaining $5 going to the casino as profit.
